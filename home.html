National Symbols of India

Unity in Diversity

National Animal: Tiger

The Bengal Tiger (Panthera tigris) symbolizes power, agility, and grace. It was declared the national animal in 1973.

National Bird: Peacock

The Indian Peacock (Pavo cristatus) represents beauty, joy, and pride. It is well-known for its colorful feathers and rhythmic dance.

National Flower: Lotus

The Lotus (Nelumbo nucifera) is a sacred flower that symbolizes purity, wealth, and knowledge despite growing in mud.
